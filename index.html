Purchasing

We buy a wide range of industrial plastic scrap.

CircleChen purchases clean and mixed plastic streams from manufacturing, assembly, and end-of-program inventories. Typical sources include edge trim, rejected parts, purge, sprues, runners, bins of obsolete stock, and automotive plastic components.

  • Recurring pickup programs or spot-lot purchasing
